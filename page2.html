Spunbond (SSS) Fabric

1

Specification

SSS’ (Spunbond – Spunbond – Spunbond) is a 3 layered 100% Polypropylene nonwoven fabric manufactured using 3 beams. This fabric not only exhibits flawless performance, supreme strength and elongation, it is also cost efficient and can be used by cost sensitive manufacturers from Hygiene and Medical industry.

2

Properties

• Strong uniform structure
• Basis weight as low as 8 gsm and as high as 80 gsm
• Right balance of softness and strength
• Multiple treatments like hydrophilicity, skin care, UV protect and    anti-static properties 

3

Applications

• Baby Care – Top sheet and Back sheet of Baby Diapers
• Adult Care - Top sheet and Back sheet of Adult incontinence          products
• Fem Care - Top sheet and Back sheet of Sanitary pads. 



Spunmelt (SSMMS) Fabric

1

Specification

SSMMS’ (3 beams of Spunbond and 2 beams of Meltblown) or simply This Nonwovens is a 5 layered 100% Polypropylene nonwoven fabric manufactured using 5 beams. This fabric offers amazing benefits like fine filtration, low pressure drop, acoustic insulation and decent strength and elongation. SMS or SSMMS fabric is widely used in the hygiene industry to make disposable diapers, feminine care products, Gowns, Coveralls, Masks and Hospital Disposables etc.

2

Properties

• Strong uniform structure
• Basis weight as low as 8gsm and as high as 80gsm
• Right balance of softness and strength
• Multiple treatments for hydrophilicity, skin care and anti-static  
• Excellent barrier properties for Liquids and Particles
• Available in multiple colours 

3

Applications

• Baby Care – Core wrap, Back sheet, Leg cuff and Ears of Baby Diapers and Adult incontinence products
• Adult Care - Core wrap, Back sheet, Leg cuff and Ears of Adult incontinence products
• Medical - Gowns, Coveralls, Mask and other Hospital Disposables etc.
